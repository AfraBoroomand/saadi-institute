Fields of Activity

The Saadi Institute works at the intersection of education, culture, and social participation. Programs are designed to be practical, academically grounded, and institutionally cooperative.

Education and Integration

  • Persian language courses for diverse learner levels
  • Teacher training and didactic support
  • Orientation and integration guidance for individuals and families

Culture, Research and Publications

  • Cultural dialogue formats and public events
  • Research collaboration with academic and civic institutions
  • Publications, translations, and knowledge-transfer resources
