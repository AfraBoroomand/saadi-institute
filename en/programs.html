Programs

Our programs combine language learning, civic orientation, and cultural participation. Each track is designed for measurable outcomes, with multilingual support and practical guidance.

Language Track

Persian language courses from beginner to advanced, including writing workshops and translation labs.

Format: Weekly classes · 10-12 weeks

Integration Track

Practical orientation modules on public services, education pathways, rights, and participation in Germany.

Format: Hybrid sessions · Individual advising

Cultural Dialogue Track

Public salons, thematic workshops, and collaborative events with partners in Berlin and beyond.

Format: Monthly events · Open registration

Enrollment Steps

  1. Review the current track overview and event calendar.
  2. Send your preferred track and language level to info@saadi-institute.de.
  3. Receive placement guidance and the next available start date.
  4. Confirm registration and receive orientation materials.

Program FAQ

Who can apply?

Programs are open to learners, families, educators, and partner institutions. Some formats are tailored by level or target group.

Are programs free?

Some offerings are free and publicly funded. Others include a modest participation fee; scholarships can be discussed during intake.

How long does placement take?

Most placement responses are sent within 3-5 business days depending on volume and language preference.
