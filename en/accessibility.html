Accessibility

We aim to provide a website that can be used across devices and diverse user needs. Accessibility improvements are part of our ongoing editorial and technical work.

Available on this site

  • Keyboard-friendly navigation and skip links
  • Readable typography and responsive layout
  • Language switching across Persian, German, and English
  • Optional controls for larger text, higher contrast, and reader mode

How to use the controls

Open the Accessibility button in the header to enable larger text, high contrast, or reader mode. Your choice is stored locally on your device and can be changed at any time.

If you notice an accessibility barrier, please contact us at info@saadi-institute.de.
