Saadi, Hafez, Schiller: Cultural Bridge

The Saadi Institute is rooted in an intellectual tradition that connects cultures through language, literature, and ethical reflection. Saadi and Hafez represent formative references from Persian literary heritage, while Schiller reflects a central humanistic reference in German cultural history.

This bridge is not symbolic only; it is implemented through educational programs, translation work, and public dialogue formats that create sustained participation across communities.
