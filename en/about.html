About

The Saadi Institute follows a humanistic approach to education and culture, connecting ethical responsibility, linguistic precision, and intercultural dialogue.

Saadi is central to this orientation: his work links moral reflection with social practice and has long been present in European debates on humanity and education.

Hafez and Schiller represent two additional points of reference: Hafez as a voice of Persian poetry, and Schiller as a symbol of German classicism and humanistic thought.

Team and Governance

Core Team

  • Program Direction: curriculum design, strategic partnerships, and quality assurance
  • Cultural Programs Lead: public events, exhibition formats, and editorial coordination
  • Operations and Community Desk: participant support, registration, and stakeholder communication

Governance Principles

  • Public-interest orientation and transparent resource use
  • Separation of program oversight and financial administration
  • Annual reporting with partner and participant feedback integration
