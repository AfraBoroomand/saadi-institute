About the Saadi Institute

The Saadi Institute was founded to build a durable framework for cultural understanding and practical dialogue between the Persian-speaking world and Germany. As an independent nonprofit in Berlin, we work at the intersection of language, culture, education, and social participation.

Our approach combines scholarship, civic education, and public culture. We create spaces where learners, families, institutions, and cultural practitioners can exchange knowledge and collaborate across communities.

Nonprofit, Independent, Transparent

The Saadi Institute is committed to public benefit, institutional transparency, and intellectual independence. All activities are aligned with educational, cultural, and social objectives rather than commercial interests.

The Saadi Institute stands for dialogue over confrontation, education over prejudice, and cultural diversity as a shared asset.
