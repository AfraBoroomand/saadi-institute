Education Programs Expanded

2026-02-02 ·

The institute introduced additional modular pathways in language learning and intercultural facilitation, including weekend workshop formats designed for working participants and families.

The updated track design combines short practice units, peer mentoring, and targeted advising sessions so participants can progress even with changing weekly schedules.

What is new

  • Weekend workshop slots for employed learners and parents
  • Integrated translation practice in intermediate modules
  • Structured advising checkpoints during each module
