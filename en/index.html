Saadi Institute gUG

A cultural bridge between the Persian-speaking world and Germany

Institute

Who we are

The Saadi Institute is a Berlin-based nonprofit cultural institution. As an independent gUG, we operate in the public interest to promote language, literature, and cultural dialogue.

Where we work

Our institutional center is Berlin, connected to partners across Germany and the Persian-speaking world.

What we do

We design educational programs, cultural initiatives, and dialogue formats in language, literature, and social participation.

Approach

Our work follows an academic and cultural approach: precise, transparent, and collaboration-driven.

Vision and Goals

  • Promote Persian language and literature in education and public life
  • Strengthen tolerance, intercultural respect, and dialogue
  • Support research, translation, and knowledge transfer
  • Advance integration through practical orientation and civic participation
  • Build partnerships with universities and institutions in Germany and beyond
